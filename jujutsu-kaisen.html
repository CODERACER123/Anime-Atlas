Jujutsu Kaisen

Yuji Itadori becomes host to the curse Sukuna and joins the Tokyo Jujutsu school to fight curses and protect humanity.

Suggested rating: TV-14 Suggested age: 14+

Core Themes

  • Facing death with purpose
  • Power and sacrifice
  • Teamwork under pressure

Story overview

Curses, sorcery, and the weight of fate.

Act 1: The cursed finger

Yuji swallows Sukuna’s finger to save his friends, becoming a vessel and entering the world of jujutsu sorcery.

Act 2: Training at Tokyo Jujutsu

Guided by Gojo, Yuji learns to control cursed energy and bonds with Megumi and Nobara.

Act 3: Curses grow stronger

The Kyoto Sister School event and Shibuya Incident reveal deadly enemies and high-stakes battles.

Finale: Choosing your path

Yuji’s resolve hardens as he confronts grief and the responsibility of carrying Sukuna’s power.

Fun facts

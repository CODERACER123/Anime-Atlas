Hunter x Hunter

Gon Freecss becomes a Hunter to find his legendary father, meeting friends and facing trials across a sprawling world.

Suggested rating: TV-14 Suggested age: 13+

Core Themes

  • Friendship and loyalty
  • Self-discovery through trials
  • Complex moral choices

Story overview

Exams, battles, and a journey to find family.

Act 1: Hunter Exam

Gon, Killua, Kurapika, and Leorio survive intense trials to earn their Hunter licenses.

Act 2: Nen awakening

The group learns Nen, a deep power system that shapes every major battle.

Act 3: Darker arcs

The Phantom Troupe and Chimera Ant arcs raise the stakes and test the heroes’ resolve.

Finale: Chasing the unknown

Gon’s journey evolves into a search for identity and purpose beyond just finding his father.

Fun facts

Trivia from the world of Hunters.

Nen is a highly detailed power system.

It includes categories like Enhancer, Emitter, and Specialist.

Arcs shift genres and tones.

The series moves from adventure to psychological drama.

The Chimera Ant arc is a fan favorite.

It’s praised for its complex villains and moral questions.
